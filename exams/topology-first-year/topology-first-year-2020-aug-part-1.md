# Topology, first year exam, August 2020, Part 1

*Work the following problems and show all work. Support all statements to the best of your ability. Answer Problems 6–10 with complete definitions or statements or proofs.*

**1.** Is it true that every continuous map \(f:[0,1]\to(0,1)\) has a fixed point. Justify your answer.

**2.** Show that every connected normal space having more than one point is uncountable.

**3.** Show that the set of algebraic numbers is countable (A number \(a\in\mathbb{R}\) is called algebraic if \(a\) is a root of a polynomial with rational coefficients).

**4.** Show that Greek letter theta \(\Theta\) is not homeomorphic to the Mercedes symbol \(S\). (Formally, \(\Theta=S^1\cup[-1,1]\times\{0\}\) and \(S=S^1\cup I_1\cup I_2\cup I_3\) are subspaces of \(\mathbb{R}^2\) where \(S^1\) is the unit circle and \(I_k\), \(k=1,2,3\), are the closed intervals in \(\mathbb{R}^2\) joining the origin with the points \((0,1)\), \(\left(\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2},-\frac12\right)\), and \(\left(-\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2},-\frac12\right)\).)

**5.** Let \(A\) be a proper subset of \(X\) and let \(B\) be a proper subset of \(Y\). If \(X\) and \(Y\) are connected, show that \((X\times Y)-(A\times B)\) is connected.

**6.** State the Extreme Value Theorem.

**7.** State the Tietze Extension Theorem.

**8.** Is the space \(I^{\mathbb{N}}\) separable? What about \(I^I\)? Here \(I=[0,1]\) and \(\mathbb{N}\) is the set of natural numbers.

**9.** Prove that the space \(X=\{(x,y)\mid xy=1\}\subset\mathbb{R}^2\) is locally compact? What is its one-point compactification?

**10.** Is every path connected space locally connected?
