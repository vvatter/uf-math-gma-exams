Topology First-Year Exam, August 2023, Part 2

Work the following problems and show all work. Support all statements to the best of your ability.

  1. Show that \(B^2\) is not homeomorphic to \(B^3\), where \(B^n\) denotes the closed unit ball in \(\mathbb{R}^n\).

  2. Show that every continuous map \(f:S^2\to S^1\) of the 2-sphere to the circle \(S^1\) is null-homotopic.

  3. Let

    \[ X=([-1,1]\times\{0\}\times\{0\})\cup(\{0\}\times[-1,1]\times\{0\})\cup(\{0\}\times\{0\}\times[-1,1])\subset\mathbb{R}^3. \]

    Show that every continuous map \(f:X\to X\) has a fixed point.

  4. Derive the Brouwer Fixed Point Theorem for \(B^2\) from the Borsuk–Ulam Theorem for \(S^2\).

  5. Let \(X\), \(Y\), and \(Z\) denote the following open rays in \(\mathbb{R}^3\):

    \[ X=(0,\infty)\times\{0\}\times\{0\},\quad Y=\{0\}\times(0,\infty)\times\{0\},\quad Z=\{0\}\times\{0\}\times(0,\infty). \]

    Compute \(\pi_1(A)\) where

    1. (a)

      \(A=\mathbb{R}^3\setminus X\)?

    2. (b)

      \(A=\mathbb{R}^3\setminus\bar X\)? Here \(\bar X\) denotes the closure of \(X\) in \(\mathbb{R}^3\).

    3. (c)

      \(A=\mathbb{R}^3\setminus(X\cup Y)\)?

    4. (d)

      \(A=\mathbb{R}^3\setminus(\bar X\cup Y)\)?

    5. (e)

      \(A=\mathbb{R}^3\setminus(X\cup Y\cup Z)\)?

    6. (f)

      \(A=\mathbb{R}^3\setminus(\bar X\cup Y\cup Z)\)?

Answer the following with complete definitions or proofs.

  1. Describe a space \(X\) with \(\pi_1(X)\cong\mathbb{Z}_3\).

  2. Show that the projective plane \(\mathbb{R}P^2\) is not homeomorphic to the surface of genus 2, \(M_2=T\#T\).

  3. Let \(X=[0,1]^{(0,1)}\) be given the product topology. Is the space \(X\)

    1. (a)

      compact?

    2. (b)

      metrizable?

    3. (c)

      separable?

  4. State the Seifert–van Kampen Theorem. Can it be used to compute \(\pi_1(S^n)\)?

  5. State the Tychonoff Theorem. Prove that every compact Hausdorff space \(X\) can be embedded into a Tychonoff cube \([0,1]^A\).
