# Analysis, PhD exam, January 2003

## Part I

*State the following theorems.*

**1.** Hölder’s inequality.

**2.** The Hahn Decomposition Theorem.

**3.** The Fubini Theorem for positive measures \(\mu\) and \(\nu\).

**4.** The Monotone Class Theorem.

**5.** The Hahn–Banach Theorem.

**6.** The Monotone Convergence Theorem.

**7.** The completeness of \(L_E^1(\mu)\).

**8.** The theorem of uniform boundedness.

## Part II

*Prove one of either Theorem 3 or 7 from part I.*

## Part III

*Solve the following problems. In problems one through four \((X,\Sigma,\mu)\) is a measure space and \(F\) is a Banach space.*

**1.** Let \(\mathcal{F}\subset\Sigma\) be a \(\sigma\)-subalgebra. Prove the existence of the conditional expectation \(E(f\mid\mathcal{F})\) for \(f\in L_F^1(\mu)\).

**2.** Let \(\mathcal{R}\) be a ring generating \(\Sigma\) and suppose \(f\in L_F^1(\mu)\). Prove, if \(\int_A f\,d\mu=0\) for each \(A\in\mathcal{R}\), then \(f=0\), \(\mu\) almost everywhere.

**3.** Suppose \(E\) (as well as \(F\)) is a Banach space and \(U\) is a bounded linear operator from \(E\) to \(F\). Show, if \(f\in L_E^1(\mu)\), then \(U\circ f\in L_F^1(\mu)\) and
\[
\int U\circ f\,d\mu=U\int f\,d\mu.
\]

**4.** Suppose \(X\) is a locally compact Hausdorff space. Show, if \(K\) is a compact set and the restriction of \(f\) to \(K\), \(f|_K\), is continuous, then \(f\phi_K\) is a Borel function. Here \(\phi_K\) is the characteristic function of \(K\).

**5.** Prove that \(C([0,1])\) is a closed proper subspace of \(L^\infty([0,1])\) (the \(L^\infty\) space with respect to Lebesgue measure on the interval \([0,1]\)). Explain how to view \(L^1([0,1])\) as a subspace of \(L^\infty([0,1])^*\). Is \(L^1([0,1])\) a proper subspace of \(L^\infty([0,1])^*\)?
