# Topology, PhD exam, May 1993

*For problems 1–5, state the following theorems. For problems 6–10, show all work and write clearly. The proofs should be detailed, leaving no doubt about the correctness!*

**1.** Baire Category Theorem

**2.** Urysohn’s Lemma

**3.** Brouwer Fixed Point Theorem

**4.** Jordan Separation Theorem for \(R^2\)

**5.** Schoenflies Theorem for \(R^2\). Does the corresponding statement hold for \(R^3\)? Explain.

**6.** What is the universal cover of \(P^2\)? Prove your answer.

**7.** Prove: Let \(C\) be the Cantor set, and let \(X\) be any compact metric space. Then there exists a continuous, surjective function \(f : C \to X\).

**8.** State and prove the Finite Simplicial Approximation Theorem.

**9.** Use the Seifert–van Kampen Theorem to compute the fundamental group of the Klein bottle.

**10.** This problem has two parts.
* (i) What is \(\widetilde{H}_0(X)\), when \(X\) is a two point space? (Do not prove.)
* (ii) Use the Mayer–Vietoris sequence and (i) to compute the homology of \(S^n\), for \(n \ge 1\).
