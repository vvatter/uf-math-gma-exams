University of Florida, Department of Mathematics

Topology PhD Exam, May 1993

PART I

State the following theorems:

  1. Baire Category Theorem

  2. Urysohn’s Lemma

  3. Brouwer Fixed Point Theorem

  4. Jordan Separation Theorem for \(R^2\)

  5. Schoenflies Theorem for \(R^2\). Does the corresponding statement hold for \(R^3\)? Explain.

PART II

For the following problems, show all work and write clearly. The proofs should be detailed, leaving no doubt about the correctness!

  1. What is the universal cover of \(P^2\)? Prove your answer.

  2. Prove: Let \(C\) be the Cantor set, and let \(X\) be any compact metric space. Then there exists a continuous, surjective function \(f:C\to X\).

  3. State and prove the Finite Simplicial Approximation Theorem.

  4. Use the Seifert–van Kampen Theorem to compute the fundamental group of the Klein bottle.

  5. This problem has two parts.

    1. (i)

      What is \(\widetilde H_0(X)\), when \(X\) is a two point space? (Do not prove.)

    2. (ii)

      Use the Mayer–Vietoris sequence and (i) to compute the homology of \(S^n\), for \(n\ge 1\).
