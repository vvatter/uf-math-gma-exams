# Complex Analysis PhD exam, May 2017

*Do six of eight. Throughout, \(\mathbb{D}=\{ |z|<1\}\) and \(\mathbb{H}=\{\operatorname{Re} z>0\}\).*

**1.** Use Liouville’s Theorem to prove the fundamental theorem of algebra.

**2.** Suppose \(f:\mathbb{C}\to\mathbb{C}\) is entire and \(|f(z)|=1\) for \(|z|=1\). What can be said about \(f\)?

**3.** Find
\[
\int_0^\pi \frac{dt}{2+\cos(t)}.
\]

**4.** Let \(\mathbb{P}=\{0<|z|<1\}\).
* (a) Does there exist an onto analytic map \(f:\mathbb{D}\to\mathbb{P}\)? If so, can \(f\) be one-one?
* (b) Find a conformal map from the strip \(S=\{0<\operatorname{Im} z<1\}\) to \(\mathbb{D}\).

**5.** Suppose \(f:\mathbb{C}\to\mathbb{C}\) is entire, non-constant and periodic of period 1, meaning \(f(z+1)=f(z)\). Prove the entire function \(g:\mathbb{C}\to\mathbb{C}\) given by \(g(z)=f(z)-z\) is onto.

**6.** Suppose \(f\) is analytic in an open set containing \(\{|z|\leq 1\}\) and \(|f(z)|<1\) for \(|z|=1\). Prove \(f(z)=z\) has exactly one solution (counting with multiplicity) in \(\mathbb{D}\).

**7.** Let
\[
\mathcal{F}=\{f:\mathbb{D}\to\mathbb{H}: f\text{ is analytic and }f(0)=1\}.
\]
Prove \(\mathcal{F}\) is a normal family.

**8.** Fix \(0<r<1\) and let \(\mathbb{A}=\{r<|z|<1\}\). Show, if \(u\) is harmonic on \(\mathbb{A}\), then there is an analytic function \(f\) on \(\mathbb{A}\) and a real constant \(c\) such that
\[
u(z)=\operatorname{Re} f(z)+c\log(|z|).
\]
